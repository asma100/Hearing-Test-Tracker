Preparing for Your Hearing Test .A quiet environment is crucial for an accurate hearing test. Background noise can interfere with the test sounds and lead to misleading results. Here's what you can do to ensure a smooth and reliable test experience:

  • Find a quiet space: Choose a location free from distractions like loud music, television, or conversations. Closing windows and doors further reduces background noise.
  • Remove hearing aids (if applicable): If you use hearing aids, please remove them before the test begins. Modern hearing aids sometimes introduce subtle background noise that can affect the test results. We will assess your natural hearing abilities without them.
  • Prepare to wear headphones: During the test, you will wear comfortable headphones or earphones that will deliver the test sounds directly to each ear. This ensures focused testing on one ear at a time.
